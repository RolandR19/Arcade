HTML5 Arcade: The Hand-Coded Portfolio

This repository stands as a curated archive of my early exploration into interactive software. Developed years ago, these projects represent the genesis of my coding journey—a time dedicated to mastering foundational logic, physics engines, and complex UI systems from scratch.

While my professional focus has since moved away from video game development, this collection remains a vital chapter of my portfolio. It documents a period of intense experimentation where every line of code was written manually, without the assistance of AI, to ensure absolute mastery over the underlying software architecture.

🏛️ Simulation & Strategy

Terra-Tech OS & Frontier OS

The Rules: Manage a futuristic agricultural colony. The player must balance three critical resources—Energy, Bio-matter, and Credits—through a complex command interface.

Mechanics: High-fidelity UI management, real-time resource calculation, and strategic planning.

TERRAGENESIS: War of Worlds

The Rules: A turn-based tactical conquest game played on a hexagonal grid. Deploy units to secure sectors and reach an 80% habitability rating to win.

Mechanics: Hex-based movement, tech-tree progression, and territory control logic.

🔺 3D Immersion (Three.js)

Meteorite Travel

The Rules: Pilot a space capsule through a dense asteroid field. Survive as long as possible by dodging or firing missiles to clear a path.

Mechanics: 3D collision detection, projectile physics, and procedural obstacle generation.

Pac-Man 3D & Pac-Man Zombie Killer

The Rules: A reimagining of the classic maze game in a first-person or isometric 3D perspective. Collect dots while outmaneuvering intelligent ghosts or zombies.

Mechanics: Pathfinding algorithms, 3D environment rendering, and wave-based difficulty.

Sphere Escape & Crossing Road

The Rules: Navigate dangerous zones, such as tunnels or busy highways, without being struck by fast-moving obstacles.

Mechanics: Timing-based movement, velocity management, and camera stabilization.

👾 2D Action & Physics (Canvas API)

Fox Platformer: The Final Quest

The Rules: A retro-inspired precision platformer. Navigate a fox through floating islands, jump on enemies, and collect gems to unlock the final chest.

Mechanics: Custom gravity engine, double-jump physics, and pixel-perfect collision detection.

Stick Figure Shooter & Space Fighting

The Rules: Defend a central position against relentless waves of enemies. Use precision aiming to eliminate targets before they breach your perimeter.

Mechanics: Mouse-tracking physics, wave spawning logic, and projectile trajectory calculation.

Skydive Escape, Avoidance & Mouse Dodge

The Rules: Pure reflex challenges. In Avoidance, survive for 20 seconds as the prey before the roles reverse and you become the hunter. In Skydive, dodge red blocks during an infinite descent.

Mechanics: Dynamic role-reversal logic, collision timers, and reaction speed testing.

🛠️ Logic, Chance & Culture

Interactive Games on Newton's Laws

The Rules: Three educational mini-games designed to demonstrate Inertia, Force ($F=ma$), and Action/Reaction through interactive physical simulations.

Mechanics: Educational physics simulation and real-time parameter adjustment.

Virtual Slot Machine, Neon Guess & Rock-Paper-Scissors

The Rules: Classic games of chance and deduction. Decode a secret number in Neon Guess or test your luck against the computer's RNG.

Mechanics: Random number generation (RNG), probability logic, and state-based victory conditions.

Mini-Vill, Catch Falling Objects & Duras Quiz

The Rules: Varied experiences ranging from collecting city items in a miniature car to testing literary knowledge of Marguerite Duras's "The Lover."

Mechanics: Top-down car physics, gravity-based object catching, and data-driven quiz structures.
